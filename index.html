Help

In Cat Catcher, your goal is to match the pictures of cats hidden under the tiles. Clicking a tile will reveal the cat underneath, then click another tile and see if it's a match. If it is a match you will get a point and the cats will remain revealed, if not, the cats will be re hidden and you can guess again. You can select how big the grid will be, and if you're looking for more of a challenge, you can turn on the guess timer.

The guess timer will add a countdown to your guess, limiting your time to think. If you don't make a guess before the timer runs out, the game will be over. Good luck!

Cat Catcher is a JavaScript game made by Sam Screpnek.
